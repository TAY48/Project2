An Informal Casual Travel Blog

I go on some trips a couple of times a year and I figured that I would start documenting my experiences. Whenever I go somewhere I eat food, drink water and breathe every second I am on earth. It is super fun to travel thousand of miles and I laugh. I also sleep when I travel. Some people may think that it is a waste of time to sleep but it's pretty good for you.

When I travel I pack clothes, bring money and stay aware. I also make sure to plan out my flight beforehand so I know what country and place I am traveling to. Otherwise it could be terrible not knowing where I am going. If I am lost or anything, I wouldn't know what to do. So I bring my cellphone with me as well since it is such a valuable resource.
